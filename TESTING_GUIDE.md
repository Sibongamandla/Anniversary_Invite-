# Website User Guide

Here is how to use and test the live Wedding Anniversary website.

## ü•Ç For Guests (The Invite Flow)
**Objective:** Unlock the invitation and submit an RSVP.

1.  **Enter the Code**: 
    *   On the landing page, click **"Unlock Invitation"**.
    *   Enter your unique invite code (e.g., `SKM2025`).
2.  **Explore**:
    *   Once unlocked, scroll down to see the "Welcome" message and Venue teaser.
    *   Visit the **Program** page for the weekend itinerary.
    *   Visit the **Guide** page for curated Cape Town recommendations.
3.  **RSVP**:
    *   Navigate to the **RSVP** page.
    *   Select **Accept**.
    *   Fill in your details (Plus One, Dietaries, Email).
    *   Click **Confirm**. You will be redirected to the Home page after a few seconds.

## üîê For Admins (The Dashboard)
**Objective:** View guest responses and headcounts.

1.  **Access the Dashboard**:
    *   Go to `/admin` (e.g., `your-website.com/admin`).
2.  **Login**:
    *   Enter your admin credentials.
    *   *(Default: `admin` / `password123`)*
3.  **Monitor**:
    *   Check the **Total Heads** card for the final count (Guests + Plus Ones).
    *   Scroll down to the **Guest Table** to see individual responses. Hover over icons to reveal dietary restrictions or notes.
